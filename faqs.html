    • FAQs | Frequently Asked Questions

    What services do you offer?

    We specialize in front-end web development, focusing on creating responsive, user-friendly, and interactive websites using modern technologies such as HTML, CSS, and Typescript. We also provide consultation on web design and user experience.


    How long does it take to develop a website?

    The timeline for developing a website varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple website can take between 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects with custom features may take several weeks or months.


    Can you redesign an existing website?

    Yes, we offer website redesign services to improve the aesthetics, functionality, and performance of your current website. We work closely with clients to ensure the updated design meets their goals.


    Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

    Yes, we offer maintenance services to ensure your website stays up-to-date, secure, and functioning smoothly. This can include updating content, fixing bugs, or implementing new features as needed.


    Will my website be mobile-friendly?

    Absolutely. We ensure all websites we develop are responsive, meaning they will adapt to different screen sizes, including mobile phones and tablets, to provide a seamless experience across all devices.


    Can you help with website hosting and domain registration?

    Yes, we can assist you in choosing the right hosting plan and registering your domain. We offer guidance on selecting the best hosting provider based on your website’s needs.


    How do I get started with a project?

    You can get started by contacting us through our website’s contact form, email, or phone. We will discuss your requirements, provide a quote, and guide you through the project initiation process.


    Do you offer e-commerce website development?

    Yes, we build e-commerce websites with secure payment integrations, user-friendly interfaces, and easy-to-manage product catalogs. We tailor e-commerce solutions to meet your business needs and scale as you grow.
